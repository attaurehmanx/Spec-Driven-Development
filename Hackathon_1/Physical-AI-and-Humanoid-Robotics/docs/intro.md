# Getting Started with Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Education Platform**. This comprehensive curriculum covers advanced robotics, ROS 2, AI integration, and practical applications in humanoid robotics.

## About This Course

This course is designed to take you from robotics fundamentals to advanced concepts in AI-powered humanoid systems. You'll learn through:

- Hands-on practical exercises
- Real-world applications
- Modern robotics frameworks
- AI algorithms and physical computing

### What You'll Learn

- ROS 2 fundamentals and advanced concepts
- Humanoid robotics principles
- AI integration in robotics systems
- Simulation environments and Isaac Sim
- Vision-Language-Action systems for robotics

## Start Learning

Begin your robotics journey by exploring the modules below, starting with Module 1: Introduction to Physical AI.

The content is structured to build your expertise progressively from fundamentals to advanced concepts. Each module contains multiple chapters with lessons, practical exercises, and projects.
